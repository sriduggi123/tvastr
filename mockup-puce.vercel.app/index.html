What is Trashformers?remove
Trashformers is a community programme for people who return containers under Goa's Deposit Refund System. You earn points, badges, and status for every container you return.
How do I earn points?add
Every container return earns points equal to the deposit value. Bonus: +5 for the first return of the week, +10 for returning at a new location.
What are levels?add
There are 5 levels: Trashformer → Bronze (50 pts) → Silver (200 pts) → Gold (1,000 pts) → Platinum (5,000 pts). You keep your level permanently.
How does Mayor work?add
The person with the most returns at a collection point in the last 30 days is the Mayor. You can see how many more returns you need to become Mayor.
Is my data safe?add
We collect only your phone number and optional display name/photo. You can delete your account at any time from Settings.
